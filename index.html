About me

I am always searching for more light and space. - Santiago Calatrava
This quote above became the beginning of my adventure with architecture. My name is Seweryn Bylinka and I am a passioner of designing at all forms, particularly I put emphasis on every detail, what is needed to acomplish the best outcomes.
Consistently I'm aiming at discovering new perspective to view is unique and andjusted. Through love of 3D modeling (also BIM), I'm able to introduce vision of each project.

Work Experiences

  • Developed and executed online, social media and print marketing strategies for the new projects.
  • Participated in all phases of the design and construction processes on 6 major projects nationally and internationally.
  • Produced drawings and layouts and assisted the clients trhoughout the projects. Clients had a satisfaction rate of over 95%
  • Created 3D models, renderings and video animations.

What I do

Our team takes over everything, from an idea and concept development to realization. We believe in traditions and incorporate them within our innovations.
All our projects incorporate a unique artistic image and functional solutions. Client is the soul of the project. Our main goal is to illustrate his/hers values and individuality through design.
